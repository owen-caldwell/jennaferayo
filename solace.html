SOLACE
2022

Greetings and Salutations friends... This zine sporadically encapsulates my definition of home. Spaces I consider to be a huge part of my growth as a human. I am a 19 year old woman and legal, but I think of myself as a grown child. I'm still a child and hope to be one for the rest of my life. This creation of mine, Solace, is a journal of physical realms I hold dearly to my heart as they signify the alignment of my inner child's harmony. The thought of these spaces set my mind at ease as I experienced an immense amount of safety, comfort, and serenity in these rooms. I have divided the zine into 4 parts and they are as follows:
1. Influence that impacted my mind's maturing in relation to these domains.
2. My room. My childhood bedroom. My indulgences plastered onto my walls. My soul's growth in this room along with other rooms in my house
3. My friend's rooms. I learned I loved I listened I cried I embraced I sowed my seeds in these rooms. I feel nothing but Peace when I reflect on my time spent in these places.
4. My parks, my fields, my trees, my neighborhood. Exploration in the days and nights where time did not exist.
