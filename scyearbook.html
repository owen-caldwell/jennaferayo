SC YEARBOOK VOL 1.
2022

I made this yearbook to give credit to the kids that push me to stay true to my ambitions. Some of these people I have known since middle school, and some of them I met minutes before I took their picture. Yet every single person in here showed out and wanted to be a part of my art without knowing what the project was even about! that's so wild 2 me... Soooo this book pays homage to you! the youth! Who I am and who I want to be as an artist, creator, and human are not feasible without the creatives that surround me. To everyone that supports me: I am forever grateful for your kindness and generosity. So, without futher ado... Drumroll please. the offcial, first-ever, top of the line, for the youth by the youth, STRAIGHT CREEPS YEARBOOK: VOLUME 1. Wow! What a delight!! As many of you may know, I am a maximalist with my work. However for this book, I (reluctantly) challenged myself to be a minimalist; Putting as much emphasis on the faces within it. I asked each participant to write down their name, age, where they are from, what they do, and any other information they would like to share with you. Yes, you. the Reader! I also told them to dress in their favorite outfits. This way, you can truly see the persona being presented to you. Each person shines a very special light. I hope you enjoy. Thx!
xoxo Jenna (SC)
